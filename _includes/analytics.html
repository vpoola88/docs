• 1. Open marketing@anthro.is google analytics account.
  • 2. Admin → click on drop down for account --> create new account.
  • 3. Fill in forms for name, website URL, etc. The website URL should be the finalized domain, not the Shopify url. Select Sports for the industry. Scroll down and Get Tracking ID once completed.
  • 4. Copy tracking script.
  • 5. Open Shopify account and select settings. Scroll down to the Google Analytics section and paste in the script.
  • 6. Double check that UA Tracking Number is the same on Google Analytics page and Shopify page.
  • 7. After 24 hours of launch, log into Google Analytics and make sure that data is being grabbed.
